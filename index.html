Simple Metronome

by Jonathan Herman

Things to try:

  • Scroll or drag up and down to adjust tempo
  • Tap the button a few times to detect the tempo you're tapping
  • This metronome works even when you don't have internet
  • On your phone or tablet, you can "add to home screen" for fullscreen mode
  • On a computer, open multiple windows of the metronome and set each to a different tempo

The source is available on GitHub
